Getting Here

Navigating your way to paradise

directions_car

By Road

A scenic 3-hour drive from Dehradun. The route takes you through Kalsi and along the Yamuna river, offering breathtaking views of the lower Shivaliks.

train

By Rail

Dehradun Railway Station is the nearest railhead. Regular taxis and private cars are available from the station directly to our campsite.

flight

By Air

Jolly Grant Airport in Dehradun is well-connected. We can arrange private luxury transfers for a seamless journey from the airport to Tipri or Lagapokri.
